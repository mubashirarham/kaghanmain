The Changing Real Estate Landscape

Current Real Estate Scenario (Pakistan 2026)

The traditional real estate brokerage model is breaking down in 2026. Commission structures are becoming highly inconsistent, and online portals have reduced the information gaps that agents once monetized. Expat Pakistanis and smart investors are no longer looking for mere "speculation" on plots; they demand **professional asset management and reliable rental yield models**.

As construction costs continue to rise, property management services and serviced apartments are growing faster than simple buying and selling. Transitioning into a tech-driven real estate entrepreneur is the only path to long-term success.
